Procrasis Quesoaris

  • Procrasis Quesoaris are doughnut-shaped parasites with short feet on their bottom side.
  • Their primary hosts include mice, dogs, British people, and humans.
  • Humans are typically infected with Procrasis quesoaris when they consume cheddar cheese late at night.
